Chapter 12 – Mobile Marketing

12.1 – Mobile vs Desktop

  • Mobile users are more impatient and click the first acceptable result.
  • Mobile screens make long reading and complex navigation stressful.
  • Mobile searches are often location-driven (e.g., looking for nearby stores).
  • Cross-device behavior (mobile → desktop or in-store purchase) complicates tracking.

12.2 – Mobile Optimization

  • Design pages to be:
    • Fast
    • Simple
    • Minimal typing
    • Clear CTAs
  • Checkout should be short and mobile-friendly.
  • Voice search is more conversational – FAQ formats and natural language help.

12.3 – Mobile Advertising & Geofencing

  • Geofencing: show ads to users inside a radius around a location (with privacy limits).
  • Used to drive local foot traffic (restaurants, stores, services).
