Chapter 10 – Social Media Platforms

Platform Summaries

  • Facebook – huge user base, strong ad targeting, weak organic reach for brands.
  • Twitter (X) – real-time short updates; educated, urban audience; good for news and support.
  • Pinterest – visual bookmarking and search; strong for DIY, recipes, lifestyle and e-commerce.
  • YouTube – long-form video; recommendation engine based on watch time and engagement.
  • LinkedIn – professional network; B2B marketing, recruiting, thought leadership.
  • Instagram – visual photos, reels, stories; influencer marketing and shopping.
  • Snapchat – ephemeral content, AR filters; younger audiences.
  • TikTok – short video, high algorithmic reach; creative, fast-paced content.
