Chapter 8 – Email Marketing & CRM

8.0 – Why Email Matters

  • Email is often the most profitable channel (low cost, high impact).
  • Relies on:
    • List capture
    • Good content
    • Deliverability (avoiding spam filters)

8.1 – Email List Capture

  • Never buy lists – leads to spam complaints and poor performance.
  • Ask for emails:
    • At checkout
    • Via pop-ups with incentives (discounts, perks)
    • On high-traffic pages with clear benefits
  • Always get explicit permission.

8.2 – Email Content & Segmentation

  • Types of emails:
    • Broadcast
    • Triggered (cart abandonment, welcome series, post-purchase)
    • Onboarding
  • Segmentation examples:
    • Gender / product interest
    • New vs returning customers
    • High- vs low-activity subscribers

8.3 – Measuring Success

  • Open rate – mostly driven by subject line.
  • CTR – clicks ÷ delivered; reflects subject + content.
  • Conversion rate – depends heavily on landing page quality.
  • AOV – average order value; email can shift product mix.
  • Churn – unsubscribes; keep acceptable but don’t hide unsubscribe link.

8.4 – Avoiding Spam Filters

  • Clean list regularly:
    • Remove hard bounces immediately.
    • Drop long-term non-openers (e.g., 3+ months).
  • Make unsubscribing easy and immediate.

8.5 – Email and CRM

  • CRM tools store customer and lead data (e.g., Salesforce).
  • Email plays a role in:
    • Generating leads
    • Nurturing leads (content sequences)
    • Supporting customers (training, updates)
