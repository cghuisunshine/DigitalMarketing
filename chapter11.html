Chapter 11 – Online Reputation Management

11.1 – Monitoring Brand Mentions

  • Watch for brand mentions on:
    • Search results
    • Review sites
    • Social media
    • News and blogs
  • Goal: make sure people see a balanced and fair picture of your brand.

11.2 – Responding to Negative Content

  • Don’t use reputation management to cover up real problems – fix them.
  • Respond professionally:
    • Acknowledge issue
    • Apologize if appropriate
    • Offer next steps or support
  • Move complex cases offline (email, phone).
  • Ignore trolls or abusive non-constructive comments.

11.3 – Creating Positive Content

  • Publish truthful, high-quality content that can rank:
    • Blog posts
    • FAQs
    • Case studies
    • Press releases
  • Goal is not to erase all negativity, but to ensure it’s balanced by strong positive information.
